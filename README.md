# OIBSIP_Task5_Social_Engineering_Attacks
Research report on social engineering attacks including phishing, pretexting, baiting, their impact, and preventive measures completed as part of the Oasis Infobyte Cybersecurity Internship.

# Social Engineering Attacks

## Objective
To study social engineering attacks and understand how attackers manipulate human behavior to gain unauthorized access to information and systems.

## Steps Performed
1. Researched common social engineering attack techniques.
2. Studied phishing, pretexting, and baiting attacks.
3. Analyzed their impact on individuals and organizations.
4. Explored prevention and awareness strategies.

## Tools Used
- Internet Research
- GitHub

## Outcome
Gained knowledge about social engineering attacks, their methods, risks, and preventive measures to improve cybersecurity awareness.
